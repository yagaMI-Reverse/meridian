PROVEN UNDER CONCURRENCY
Append-only ledger
No UPDATE/DELETE grants + a trigger that stops even superusers. History is immutable.
Idempotent retries
Stripe-style Idempotency-Key: replays return the original response, mismatches get 422.
PostgreSQL RLS
Deny-by-default policies keyed to a per-transaction tenant GUC from the verified JWT.
Row-lock serialization
SELECT … FOR UPDATE queues concurrent writers per account — balances can't interleave.
Reconciliation job
Hourly + on-demand sweeps compare balances vs SUM(ledger) and report drift to the cent.
Guards · pipes · interceptors
JWT + RBAC guards, DTO validation pipes, logging & serialization interceptors, Prisma filters.
